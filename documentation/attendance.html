Attendance

Track and manage student and teacher attendance records.

Overview

The Attendance section allows you to view, add, edit, and import attendance records for students and teachers. Attendance data is crucial for reporting and compliance.

Viewing & Managing Attendance
  1. Go to the Attendance section from the sidebar.
  2. Browse attendance records by date, school, or class.
  3. Use filters to narrow down records by student, teacher, or event.

Adding, Editing & Importing Attendance
  1. Click "Add Attendance" to create a new record.
  2. To edit, click the "Edit" button next to a record and update the details.
  3. To import, click the "Import" button and upload your CSV or Excel file.
  4. Map columns to the correct fields and confirm import.
