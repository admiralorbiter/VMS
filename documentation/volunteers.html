Managing Volunteers

Everything you need to know about volunteer management in the VMS.

Overview

The Volunteer Directory lets you add, import, search, edit, and manage all volunteers in your system. Use advanced search, sort, and pagination to quickly find and organize your volunteer data.

  • Edit, add, and delete volunteers (admin only for delete)
  • Import volunteers from spreadsheets
  • Search by name, organization, skills, email, and more
  • Advanced search and column sorting
  • Pagination for large datasets
Adding a New Volunteer
  1. Navigate to Volunteer Directory: Click "Managing Volunteers" in the sidebar.
  2. Click "Add New Volunteer": Use the "Add" button at the top of the page.
  3. Fill in Required Information: Enter name, email, and any additional details.
  4. Add Professional Details: Include organization, role, and skills.
  5. Save the Record: Click "Save" to add the volunteer.

Name and email address are required. Other fields are optional but recommended for better matching with events.

Importing Volunteers from Spreadsheet
  1. Prepare Your Spreadsheet: Ensure your Excel or CSV file has columns for name, email, and other volunteer data.
  2. Access Import Function: In Volunteer Directory, click the "Import" button.
  3. Upload Your File: Select your spreadsheet and click "Upload".
  4. Map Columns: Match your spreadsheet columns to system fields.
  5. Review and Import: Check the preview and click "Import Data".

Always backup your data before importing. Duplicate email addresses will be flagged for review.

Searching and Filtering Volunteers

Quick Search Options:

  • Name: Type any part of first or last name
  • Organization: Find volunteers from specific companies
  • Skills: Search by volunteer expertise
  • Email: Locate specific volunteer by email
  • Local Status: Filter by volunteer availability

Advanced Search: Click "Advanced Search" to combine multiple criteria and create complex queries for targeted volunteer recruitment.

Editing and Deleting Volunteers
  1. Edit: Click the "Edit" button next to a volunteer to update their information.
  2. Delete: (Admin only) Use the "Delete" button to remove a volunteer from the system.
