# Impact & KPI Reports

These reports provide high-level metrics for grant reporting and district feedback.

## District Impact Report

**Location**: `Reports > District Impact` (or similar)

Shows the value provided to specific school districts.

### Key Metrics
-   **Unique Students Reached**: Computed from event attendance rosters.
-   **Unique Volunteers**: Count of distinct volunteers who served the district.
-   **Total Hours**: Sum of volunteer hours delivered to the district.
-   **Unique Organizations**: Number of partner companies engaged.

## Organization Participation

**Location**: `Reports > Organization Participation`

Shows engagement levels of partner companies.

### Features
-   **Ranking**: Sort by most active organizations.
-   **Drill-down**: Click an organization to see its individual volunteers.
