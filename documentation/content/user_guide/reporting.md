# Reporting & Dashboards

This guide covers how to generate reports to measure impact and recognize volunteers.

## 1. Available Dashboards

Access reports via the **Reports** menu.

### Volunteer Recognition
-   **Purpose**: Identify top volunteers for thank-you messages.
-   **Metrics**: Total Hours, Total Events, Most Recent Activity.
-   **Actions**: Export to CSV for mail merge.

### Organization Participation
-   **Purpose**: See which partner companies are most engaged.
-   **Metrics**: Unique volunteers per org, Total hours per org.

### District Impact
-   **Purpose**: Reporting to school districts.
-   **Metrics**: Unique students reached, Schools served, Teacher completion rates.

## 2. Ad Hoc Reporting

For specific questions not covered by dashboards:
1.  Go to **Reports > Ad Hoc**.
2.  Use the **Query Builder** to select criteria (e.g., "All volunteers from Acme Corp in 2025").
3.  **Run Query** and Export.

## 3. Partner Reconciliation

Use this to match external lists (e.g., KCTAA members) against our database.
1.  Go to **Reports > Partner Match**.
2.  Upload the partner CSV list.
3.  System highlights **Exact Matches** and **Fuzzy Matches** (similar names).
4.  Confirm matches and export the "Active Volunteers" list.
