Business Rules Validation
Comprehensive business logic validation for events

Status Transitions:

  • Draft → Requested → Confirmed → Published → Completed
  • Any status can transition to Cancelled
  • Invalid transitions are blocked (e.g., Completed cannot go back to Draft)

Data Consistency:

  • End date must be after start date
  • Attended count cannot exceed registered count
  • Participant counts must be non-negative

Workflow Validation:

  • Event creation workflow with required field progression
  • Capacity planning and volunteer assignment validation
  • Skills and requirements management
Implemented Comprehensive business rules with status transitions and data consistency
