Performance Metrics

Understanding Performance Metrics
What These Metrics Mean
  • Execution Time: How long validation processes take to complete
  • Memory Usage: System memory consumed during validation
  • CPU Usage: Processor utilization during validation runs
  • Records Processed: Total number of records validated
Performance Benchmarks
  • Good: < 5 seconds, < 100MB memory
  • Fair: 5-15 seconds, 100-500MB memory
  • Poor: > 15 seconds, > 500MB memory

Trend Analysis & Anomaly Detection

Understanding Trends & Anomalies
Trend Analysis
  • Improving: Quality scores are getting better over time
  • Declining: Quality scores are getting worse over time
  • Stable: Quality scores remain consistent
  • Fluctuating: Quality scores vary significantly
Anomaly Detection
  • Statistical Outliers: Unusual patterns in your data
  • System Changes: Impact of updates or modifications
  • Data Issues: Sudden quality problems
  • Seasonal Patterns: Regular variations in quality

Quality Scoring Settings

Understanding Quality Scoring Settings
What You Can Configure
  • Entity Weights: How much each entity type contributes to overall score
  • Validation Type Weights: Importance of different validation categories
  • Quality Thresholds: Minimum acceptable quality scores
  • Custom Rules: Entity-specific validation requirements
Best Practices
  • Critical Fields: Give higher weights to essential data
  • Business Impact: Align weights with business priorities
  • Regular Review: Update settings as requirements change
  • Documentation: Keep track of custom configurations
