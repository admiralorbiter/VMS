Required Columns
  • UserAuthId - Unique Pathful user ID

Auto-Linking

Profiles will automatically link to existing TeacherProgress and Volunteer records that were imported via Session Report (matching by Pathful User ID).

How It Works
What Gets Imported
  • Educators - Teachers registered in Pathful
  • Professionals - Volunteers/speakers in Pathful
  • Email addresses and contact info
  • School and district information
  • Job titles and skills
  • Activity metrics (login counts, last session)
What Gets Skipped
  • Students - Not needed for tracking
  • Parents - Not needed for tracking
  • Rows without valid UserAuthId
